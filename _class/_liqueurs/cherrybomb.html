Cherry Bomb

Directions

1) Place cherries (drained), mixed with everclear into a sealable cannister for approx. 2 1/2 months.

2) Serve by the shot.

Extra) To drown out flavor of everclear, put one cherry in shot glass, and fill with sprite or 7up. Take cherry and soda into mouth at same time, chew cherry, and swallow all together. Enjoy!
