Alien Orgasm

Directions

1) Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker half-filled with ice cubes.

2) Shake well, and strain into a collins glass.

3) Garnish with a slice of orange, and serve.

Ingredients

* 1/4 oz Malibu® coconut rum

* 1/4 oz Blue Curacao liqueur

* 1/4 oz Tia Maria® coffee liqueur

* 1/4 oz Bailey's® Irish cream
