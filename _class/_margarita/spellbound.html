Directions

1) Rim martini glass with sprinkles.

2) Shake with ice, strain into prepared martini glass, and garnish with a lime wedge.

* 2 parts Pinnacle Atomic Hot Vodka

* 1 part DeKuyper Pucker Sour Apple Schnapps
