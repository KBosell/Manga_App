Crazy Cat

Directions

1) In a shaker put ice, add both rums, one splash of 7-up or Sprite, 2 splashes of cranberry juice and shake.

2) Pour into a large cocktail glass, garnish with a cherry.

Ingredients

* 3 oz Captain Morgan® spiced rum

* 3 oz Malibu® coconut rum

* 1 splash 7-Up® soda

* 2 splashes cranberry juice
